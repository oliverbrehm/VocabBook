A useful feature of Vocab Book is the ability to look a word up from within the app.
First enter the new word in its textfield (alternatively you can leave the "word" textfield empty and enter only a translation) and then click on "lookup".


When you first look a word up in a set, google will be used as a default. But you can actually use any other webpage for lookup, such as a dictionary page.
To choose another page, click on "Settings".


On the next page, you will see some example pages you can use. Choose a Englisch - Spain dictionary for example.


After you chose a page, all further lookups in this set will use that page. Of course you can change the page again any time in settings.
If you also entered a translation when first creating the word, you don't have to actually click in the web page's search bar. Instead, you use the "Word" and "Translation" buttons, to enter the appropriate content automatically.


Instead of the proposed pages, you can also add your onw in settings. Click on "Add URL"."


To add a page, you have to know its internet adress (URL).
First, enter a description for the page (1).
Next, you need the page's URL.
Click on "Add" to finish the process and the web page will be displayed as an option in the previous page.
