When learning a new language, you need one thing above all: words and more words!
So how do you manage to learn those efficiently?

Vocab Book gives you the opportunity to always have your words with you.
Studying vocabulary is possible in any location, can even be fun with the app's quiz and test mode.
Use Vocab Book on all your iOS devices, iCloud will automatically keep your words in sync. Adding new words is easy and fast.

*** Vocab Book Features ***

- Quickly create as many words as you need, sorted into different vocabulary sets
- Progress monitored in a simple, efficient system
- Easy studying by automatic quizzing
- Automatic iCloud synchronisation
